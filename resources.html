General Information about Domestic Violence

Domestic violence is a pattern of behavior in a relationship that is used to gain or maintain power and control over an intimate partner. It can take many forms, including physical violence, emotional abuse, and financial manipulation.

Statistics and Facts

  • 1 in 4 women and 1 in 9 men experience severe intimate partner physical violence.
  • Domestic violence affects individuals regardless of race, ethnicity, or socioeconomic status.
  • In 70% of cases, domestic violence is witnessed by children, leading to long-term emotional and psychological effects.

Safety Planning Tips

  • Identify safe places to go if you need to leave quickly.
  • Keep important documents and essentials in a safe and accessible location.
  • Develop a code word with friends or family to signal when you need help.
  • Have a backup plan, including a way to communicate with trusted individuals.
